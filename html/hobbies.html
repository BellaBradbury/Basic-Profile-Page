Hobbies

THEATER

When I'm not hard at work on a web project, I can usually be found pursuing an artistic project where I can sharpen my creative, adaptive, and communicative skills.

I've enjoyed acting since preschool where I was a part of my school's christmas play, How the Grinch Stole Christmas. Since then I have accrued over two decades of experience and have performed in well over 150 productions. Most recently I have been performing in semi-professional projects both onscreen and onstage and always look forward to what the future holds.

READING

I most enjoy relaxing when it involves getting cozy with a hot tea and a great book! My favorite genre has always been historical fiction, though over the years I have also come to enjoy more factual pieces.

Stories I keep coming back to:

  • The Giver Quartet (Lois Lowry)
  • Little Women (Louisa May Alcott)
  • The Nancy Drew Series (Carolyn Keene)
  • The Complete Adventures of the Borrowers (Mary Norton)
